PawSwap FAQ

How is PawSwap Network different from a DEX?

A single DEX provides one pool, while PawSwap Network aggregates multiple pools and RFQ makers to deliver the best net outcome.

Does PawSwap require custody of funds?

No, swaps are executed on-chain via PawSwap Chain. You keep full control of your wallet and assets.

How can I reduce failed trades?
